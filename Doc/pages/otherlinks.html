Other Kinds of Links

You can link to other things besides passages. For example, if you wanted to split your story into several parts, or just link to a reference, you can specify a Web address instead:

:: Start
It's a dark and stormy night aboard the [[Orient Express|http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orient_Express]]. You can't sleep; something about the motion of the train disturbed you subtly. So instead you have elected to spend the night in the dining car, sipping coffee and perusing the newspaper.

To do this, you type a vertical bar... that's a |, which is shift-backslash on American keyboards... between your text and the address you want to point to. External addresses appear with a solid underline in your story, to distinguish them from normal links.

You can also make a link to an e-mail address by putting ''mailto:'' in front of it, like this:

:: Example
Tell it to [[the chief|mailto:president@whitehouse.gov]].
